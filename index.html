NPC Game

The game was built in Unity 2021.3.15f1. For the purpose of convenience, it became an import model of New York, respectively the enemy from Mixamo and a drone as a companion. The companion NPC designed has 4 states (functionalities)

  • Idle mode-> spins around the player when the player is also not moving, at a random point
  • Follow mode-> when the player moves, the drone follows the player from the last point it was before
  • Attack Mode-> launch various particles when the closest enemy is within the attack radius
  • Health PickUp mode-> by pressing the L button of the keyboard, the drone goes to the nearest healthpack, collects it and follows the player again.
